Important Background Information

Please read this page in detail

The following terms will be used frequently in this study, so please familiarize yourself with them.

Google Play Store:

The Google Play Store is the official platform for Android users to discover, download, and manage mobile applications. When visiting the Google Play Store, users are presented with a wealth of information about each app (e.g., app description, ratings, data safety, etc.) to help them make informed decisions.

Privacy Transparency Channels:

The Google Play Store provides multiple interfaces for app developers to communicate their apps' privacy and security practices. Our study focuses on mobile apps' privacy practices (e.g., data collection, storage, and sharing, etc.), and an app user can learn about them mainly through the data safety, privacy policy, and permission manifest interfaces provided by Google Play Store for an android app. We refer to these three interfaces as three Privacy Transparency Channels in this study.

Data Safety:

The Google Play Store provides the data safety privacy transparency channel for an app developer to display an overview about what personal information is collected by the app and how the collected information will be used, etc.
An example of the data safety privacy transparency channel for the Google Home mobile app is shown in Fig.1.

Privacy Policy:

The Google Play Store provides the privacy policy (privacy) transparency channel for an app developer to provide an URL link of the app's privacy policy, which explains how the app collects, uses, stores, and protects app users' personal information, etc.
An example of the privacy policy transparency channel for the Google Home mobile app is shown in Fig.2.

Permission Manifest:

The Google Play Store provides the permission manifest privacy transparency channel for an app developer to display the system permissions (e.g., access to the camera, microphone, contacts, GPS location, etc.) that the app may require.
An example of the permission manifest privacy transparency channel for the Google Home mobile app is shown in Fig.3.
